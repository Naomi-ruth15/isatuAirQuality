About Our Mission

ISAT U Ambiance Project

The ISAT U Ambiance Project develops and deploys indoor air quality (IAQ) monitoring devices in select ISAT University buildings. These devices monitor key ambient parameters: Total Volatile Organic Compounds (TVOC), equivalent carbon dioxide (eCO2), humidity, temperature, and dust density. A real-time dashboard displays this data, calculating and presenting an Indoor Air Quality Index (IAQI) to reflect the current air quality status. This continuous IAQ assessment aims to provide data-driven insights on how to improve student health and well-being by identifying and addressing potential environmental concerns within the university's learning spaces. This provides a continuous assessment of the IAQ within the monitored spaces.

Air Quality Parameters

We monitor critical environmental factors that impact health, cognitive performance, and indoor comfort.

🌫️

TVOC

Gases emitted from paints, cleaning products, and building materials that impact air quality and may cause health problems.

🟢

eCO₂

Equivalent CO₂ derived from VOC levels, used to estimate indoor air freshness and ventilation adequacy.

💧

Humidity

Tracks moisture in the air. Extremes can cause mold growth or respiratory discomfort.

🌡️

Temperature

Affects comfort, cognitive performance, and health. Balanced indoor temps promote wellness.

🌪️

Dust Density

Measures airborne particles that may carry allergens or pollutants, impacting breathing and indoor hygiene.

Framework, Monitoring Index & Research Partnership

Framework

We aim to develop a sustainable and adaptive framework based on IAQ data, building use, and green building codes. This guides optimal design and retrofitting for air quality and well-being.

Monitoring Index

The IAQ Monitoring Index integrates real-time sensor data and AI to assess indoor air. User feedback enhances accuracy and informs immediate actions.

Research Partnership

We consult and coordinate with SUCs to develop monitoring databases and IAQ indexing, aligning with UNSD goals for sustainability.
