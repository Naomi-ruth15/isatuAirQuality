Our Mission

Creating a healthier learning environment through data-driven indoor air quality monitoring.

The ISAT U Ambiance Project develops and deploys indoor air quality (IAQ) monitoring devices in select ISAT University buildings.

These devices monitor key ambient parameters: Total Volatile Organic Compounds (TVOC), equivalent carbon dioxide (eCOâ‚‚), humidity, temperature, and dust density.

A real-time dashboard displays this data and calculates an Indoor Air Quality Index (IAQI) to reflect the current air quality status within university buildings.

This ongoing assessment provides data-driven insights to improve student health and well-being by identifying and addressing environmental issues in learning spaces.
